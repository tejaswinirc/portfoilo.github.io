Project 1: CARDIAC HEALTH ASSESSMENT FOR VEHICLE DRIVERS: ECG MONITORING ANDHEART DISEASE DIAGNOSIS USING IOT AND MACHINE LEARNING

My Project Cardiac Health Assessment System for Vehicle Drivers leverages the integration of Machine Learning (ML) and the Internet of Things (IoT) to ensure continuous monitoring and analysis of drivers' cardiovascular well-being. Employing smart wearables equipped with ECG sensors, the system captures real-time heart health data during driving and non-driving periods. Through IoT connectivity, this information is seamlessly transmitted to a centralized platform. Advanced ML algorithms analyze the ECG patterns, enabling the early detection of irregularities and potential risks. In case of abnormalities, the system issues immediate alerts, facilitating prompt intervention and, if necessary, remote medical consultations. The system's user-friendly interface provides drivers with access to their health data and personalized recommendations, fostering proactive health management. This innovative approach not only enhances driver safety by identifying potential cardiac issues but also contributes to a data- driven understanding of the relationship between cardiovascular health and driving conditions.
